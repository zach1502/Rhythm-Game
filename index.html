Welcome to my rhythm game! This is a project I've been working on for a while. Unfortunately for the mobile users, this game is not playable for mobile devices!

How To Play:

- Tap corresponding keys on your keyboard to the beat of the music

- Keybinds are labeled on the keys themselves. Labels will be hidden when the game starts.

- Get more points for hitting notes closer to the center of the keys

If you have epilepsy or are sensitive to flashing lights, I HIGHLY RECOMMEND disabling the visual effects in the options panel.
